Aether-Touched

Home

Demigods like creatures, gifted with heightened sensitivity to the world's subtle energies. This sensitivity manifested as an innate talent for magic, exceptional longevity, and piercing insight. Their celestial heritage was evident in their striking appearance - pale, almost luminous skin, and eyes that seemed to hold the depth of the night sky.

An Aether-Touched can live for thousands of years if left undisturbed with the longest lived Aether-Touched having an unknown lifespan. An Aether-Touched tend to be small creatures often only 4-5 feet tall and slight of build. Historians who have encountered Aether-Touched remark on their almost child like appearance and strange attitudes and fixations.
