Kyaelx is the unique martial art wielded by the Aether-Touched involving teleportation as well as attacking and defending the soul. Kyaelx has 2 main branches:

Aetherian Kyaelx

Aetherian Kyaelx is as much a dance as it is a style of martial art with a focus on balanced offensive and defensive steps in along side your opponent. Little else is known about it.

Post-Aetherian Kyaelx

Post-Aetherian Kyaelx is an art of raw practicality, being brutally efficient in its offensive capability with little defense or no defense. It developed out of a need for survival and dominance in the civil war that cemented the collapse of The Aetherian.
