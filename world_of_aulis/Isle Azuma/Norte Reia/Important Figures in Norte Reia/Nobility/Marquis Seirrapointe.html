Marquis Esteban Nierro, known as 'The Border Lord', rules March Seirrapointe, a small but strategically vital borderland of Norte Reia. Marquis Nierro is renowned for his expertise in frontier management and diplomacy.

House Seirrapointe

House Seirrapointe, despite its small territory, wields significant influence due to its control over a crucial section of Norte Reia's western border. Their power stems from their unparalleled knowledge of border dynamics and their ability to manage cross-border relations effectively.

Key Assets

  • The Sentinel's Chain: A network of signal towers for rapid border communication
  • Puerto Oculto: A discreet port for monitoring coastal traffic and facilitating covert trade
  • The Border Wardens: An elite force of rangers skilled in frontier patrolling

Challenges

  1. Balancing strict border control with facilitating trade
  2. Managing relationships with neighboring powers
  3. Combating smuggling without antagonizing criminal organizations
  4. Ongoing dispute with Arj County over lead mining rights in a border zone

Family Members

  2. Lady Catalina Seirrapointe (48): Wife, from a Zepor merchant family, skilled in economic negotiations
  3. Rodrigo Seirrapointe (26): Eldest son, captain of the Border Wardens
  4. Isabela Seirrapointe (24): Eldest daughter, family diplomat
  5. Diego Seirrapointe (19): Younger son, studying at the Studium of Zepor
  6. Sofia Seirrapointe (16): Youngest daughter, interested in border region flora and fauna
  7. Don Alvaro Seirrapointe (47): Esteban's brother, Castellan of Castillo Robledal
