Miromori Forest is the place a angered parent may threaten to send there child to. It is a dangerous place home to many hostile creatures.

The Mirian Lion

Weighing more than 2 tons, standing as tall as a moose, and being more of a tank on legs than an animal is the Mirian Lion. It is said to be virtually unkillable with supernatural powers of regeneration. It its also said that the Mirian Lion smell blood from miles away and is as fast as a raging bull. ![[Pasted image 20240604152035.png]]

The Wendigo

A spirit born from cannibalism the wendigo is a fierce creature that even the most experienced of monster hunters fear.
