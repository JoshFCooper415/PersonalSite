[[Peridia|Peridia]] is an old elven city and home to the Peridi Elves. It was long a highly secretive city state but about 200 years ago Good King Dectus began diplomatic relationships with the nations of Isle Azuma. The elves of [[Peridia|Peridia]] have a darker skin tone and dark eyes, uncommon amongst the high elves from which they descend. Modern Peridia has few true elves, but is flush with those who claim some elven heritage.

Nobility in Peridia

The social system of Peridia is dependent on heritage with the descendance of Peridi Elves having the highest social standing with ones status dependent on how long there family has lived in Peridia. This is enforced by the king of Peridia, the long reigning Good King Dectus.

Geography and Architecture:

Peridia is a coastal city nestled between a dense jungle and the sea. The city is built in tiers, with older structures closer to the water and newer developments climbing up the gentle slopes inland. The architecture blends elven aesthetics with practical adaptations to the tropical climate, featuring open-air structures, wide eaves to protect from rain, and natural ventilation. Canals and waterways crisscross the city, serving both as transportation routes and a natural cooling system.

Tidebinders Library

The Tidebinders Library is Peridia's most renowned institution, housing centuries of accumulated knowledge, it is one of if not the oldest library on Isle Azuma. The library is known for its extensive collection of historical texts, natural philosophies, and star charts. Scholars from across Isle Azuma seek permission to study here.
