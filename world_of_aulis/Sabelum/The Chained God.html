Aka: The Enslaved God The Prisoner

The Chained God is less a god than a slave, the followers of the chained god believe the deity to be in there dept for their faith and in exchange it must give them power. Worship, if it can even be called that, of The Chained God is highly illegal the world over save Sabelum where it is the primary religion. The worshipers of The Chained God are seen as petulant children by many but the implication of their dogmatic faith that a deity should be owned and controlled is a terrifying notation to god-fearing priests and men the world over.

Depiction

The Chained God is often depicted as a giant man-like hunched creature with large tusks and no face, chained to stones at the in a deep cave.

Subversive worship

Not all who worship The Chained God believe it is the deity who owes them, some believe and argue for the unchaining of there god. These worshipers are very rare but The Chained God is happy to grant power to those seeking to free him.
